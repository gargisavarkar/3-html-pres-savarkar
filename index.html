783 MILLION PEOPLE LIVE BELOW 1.90 A DAY.

PICTURE IT..

You need to feed your family, travel to work, and get your child school supplies! But you only have 1.90 in your pocket. Sems quite impossible, but for more than 700 million people, thats normal. 700 MILLION people live in extreme poverty. Yes. The overwhelming majority of people are still living on 1.90 or LESS. Could you imagine? How would you get those new pairs of shoes or that new iphone XR! These first world problems are far out of the minds of those in poverty.

WHY DOES IT MATTER?

Poverty is SO much more than just lack of income and resources. It includes hunger, limited access to education and social discrimination. Normal, everyday people don't have to deal with hunger or lack of discrimination. But just think, put yourself in their shoes. Causes of poverty are changing trends in a country's economy. Extreme weather changes can also cause this problem.

POVERTY IS MORE THAN NO MONEY

Well why should I care? You may ask. I'm not poor. Well poverty affects EVERYONE. Nearly all possible consequences of poverty have an impact on children. Yes, children. Kids like me and you! Poor infastructures, unemployment and lack of income reflect on kid's education, malnutrition, child labor, and diseases of all kinds. Think about this. What if you were in this situation. What would YOU do?
